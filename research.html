research

For my research topic, I wanted to delve in to the sensitive topic of abuse towards nurses. As a future nurse with an RN as my mom, I understand that nurse abuse is an issue that is not discussed enough. At some point in time, this issue has become a normal, anticipated part of being a nurse as most nurses can expect to be abused by their patients (and even other coworkers). Below, I included some interesting articles/studies about nurse abuse. These sources stood out to me the most and contain many details about why it happens, who it most likely will affect, and the negative consequences of experiencing nurse abuse.

Sources:

  • An overview of violence towards nurses
  • Unreported cases of violence
  • Prevalence of violence towards psychiatric nurses
