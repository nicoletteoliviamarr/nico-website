my hobbies

Reading has been a major hobby of mine since I was a kid. I always have a book in progress and love going on reading dates at the creek. I love an engaging story, both fiction and non-fiction. Even better, I love discussing books with my friends and family.

Doing nails is a newer hobby I've gotten into. I've always had a different polish each week and stepped up my game once I started experimenting with gel polish. Currently, I've been practicing GelX and getting better at doing designs.

I picked up the guitar a couple of years ago. My favorite thing about guitar is that I can use whatever chords I like and make my own picking pattern to sing to. I may not be perfect at it, but signing and playing brings me joy. I find it fun to experiment with different songs or to learn a challenging picking pattern.
